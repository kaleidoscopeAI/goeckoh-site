Why the name “Goeckoh”? It’s how “Go Echo” sounded when a neurodivergent individual said it—so the name itself starts with the voice we’re protecting.

What Go Echo does

Many tools replace the user’s voice with a generic synthetic voice. Go Echo preserves identity.

Listen

Captures speech naturally, without demanding perfect timing.

Correct

Gently rewrites speech while keeping intent intact.

Replay

Plays the corrected phrase back in the user’s own voice, always first person.
