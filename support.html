Support

Fast support starts with the right details.

Contact

Email: support@goeckoh.com (replace with your real support email).

Tip: Create this email in your domain settings or through your mail provider.

When you email support, include

  • Device type (Windows/macOS/Linux/Android)
  • Headphones or speakers used
  • Microphone model (or “built‑in”)
  • What you expected vs what happened
  • Screenshots if possible

Common fixes

  • No sound: set the correct output device, then restart the app.
  • Mic missing: unplug/replug, reselect mic, restart.
  • Choppy replay: switch to lower‑quality mode or try a faster device.
Billing: If you purchased via Stripe, include the email used at checkout so support can locate the transaction.
